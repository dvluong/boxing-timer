Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

JabClock ("we", "our", or "the app") is a boxing round timer for iOS. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

JabClock does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect any personal information directly. All your timer settings, presets, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

JabClock displays ads through Google AdMob to keep the app free. AdMob may collect certain data for the purpose of serving relevant advertisements, including:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA), only if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Coarse location (IP-based, not GPS)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

If you decline the tracking permission prompt, AdMob will serve contextual (non-personalized) ads and will not access your advertising identifier.

For more details on Google's data practices, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

Ad Attribution

JabClock uses Apple's SKAdNetwork framework for privacy-preserving ad attribution. This does not identify you personally and is managed entirely by Apple.

Data Storage

All app data (timer configurations, saved presets, sound preferences, display settings) is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data is not backed up to any external server, is not shared with third parties, and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect: names, email addresses, phone numbers, health or fitness data, location data, photos, contacts, browsing history, or any other personal information.

Children's Privacy

JabClock is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us so we can address the situation.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into JabClock is Google AdMob for advertising. No other third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services are used.

Your Choices

You can control ad personalization by adjusting the App Tracking Transparency setting for JabClock in your device's Settings app under Privacy & Security > Tracking. You can also reset your advertising identifier or enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
